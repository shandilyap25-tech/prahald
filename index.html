Under the Hood

The Transformer
Architecture

Three core mechanisms power every modern LLM.

01

Tokenization

Text is broken into sub-word tokens — the atomic units the model actually processes. "Running" → ["Run", "##ning"].

02

Self-Attention

Each token attends to every other token in the sequence, dynamically weighting which words matter most for context.

03

Next-Token Prediction

Trained on hundreds of billions of words, the model learns to predict the most likely next token given the full context.
